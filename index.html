Solution

Kshetrajiva offers an integrated animal detection and deterrence system that combines sensors, water and chemical sprays, and camera technologies to prevent wild animals from entering farmlands.

Key Features:

  • Solar-powered spray systems to deter animals using a mix of water and natural chemical repellents.

Technology Overview

Sensor and Control Systems:

The system uses Reolink RLC-423 cameras for animal detection, powered by YOLOv3 deep learning algorithms for real-time detection.

Spray Mechanism:

A 90% water and 10% chemical mixture is sprayed to repel animals, optimizing water and chemical use. It operates automatically based on signals from the sensor unit.
